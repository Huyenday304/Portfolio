2 Weeks

Lecturer: Giang Nguyen

Project Overview

The Jenga Tower by MikaMarvel is a poem that reveals the hidden weaknesses of the well-known game Jenga through simple yet metaphorical language. This project emphasizes the stark contrasts within Jenga’s nature: emptiness and solidity, stillness and trembling, strength and weakness, pretense and concealment. Through the medium of an interactive web-based poem, The Jenga Tower conveys a message of self-awareness, encouraging the audience to acknowledge their flaws and seek support rather than keep them hidden.
