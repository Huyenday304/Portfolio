    LINH ĐÌNH

    Type

    Type Design

    Duration

    2 Weeks (2024)

    Course

    Type & Design

    Mentor

    Giang Nguyen

    Brief

    A display typeface based on 3 chosen keywords. Design a typographic poster using the typeface to introduce the typeface, the glyph set, with typeface's name included.

    Idea

    LINH ĐÌNH is a display typeface inspired by the beautiful curves of Vietnamese village temples and traditional wedding costumes. With the elegant curves and the stroke contrast, it presents the beauty of culture and tradition in the modern age.
