    Nonsense

    Type

    Stop Motion

    Duration

    1 Month

    (2023)

    Course

    Creative Thinking and Innovation

    Mentor

    Manny Ling

    Brief

    A creative and provoking stop-frame animation based on 20 provocative words from a favorite written passage. The intention should be to challenge the audience to look at animation as a visual spectacle. Then develop an outline proposal for the display of the animation in a space other than a cinema. A space we will call a 'not cinema'.

    Idea

    Marine is a non-sense stop-motion with no narrative, which serves the purpose of expanding creativity and innovation to the utmost with home-found & organic materials, bringing new life, new roles to old objects. It is built from 20 evocative words: hammock, frightened, mad, displeased, flash, air, utmost, unsatisfactory, swinging, lovely, dream, ocean, green, peace, voice, quieted, neglect, tossing, meshes, castles.

    A "Not Cinema" space - an aquarium where the animation's projected onto the fish tank. When people stand by the giant fish tanks to see the animation, one can see the contrast among the scale of human and nature, the finity and infinity.
