Nonsense

Stop Motion / 2023

1 Month

Lecturer: Manny Ling

Project Overview

Marine is a non-sense stop-motion with no narrative, which serves the purpose of expanding creativity and innovation to the utmost with home-found & organic materials, bringing new life, new roles to old objects. It is built from 20 random words, tranformed into a morph-board, a stop-frame animation then a "Not Cinema" space - an aquarium where the animation's projected onto the fish tank. When people stand by the giant fish tanks to see the animation, one can see the contrast among the scale of human and nature, the finity and infinity.
