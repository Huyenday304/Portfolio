    Ladybud

    Type

    User Experience

    User Interface

    Duration

    3 Months

    (2024)

    Course

    User Experience Stream

    Client: Bayer Vietnam

    Mentor

    Becky Lu

    Brief

    Research and development of digital solutions that align with high-quality farming model of Bayer in Vietnam. Design Challenge: Generate and design an idea for an interactive experience for a user-friendly platform, it can include both digital and physical experience.

    Idea

    Ladybud is an assistant device paired with an app to help farmers in Ho Chi Minh City monitor the farm and give daily reports. It can move around on its own and rotate 360 degrees then notify the farmers about the real-time pest and disease. This is designed specifically for those who encounter resistant pests and rely heavily on chemicals. It provides different methods to treat the insects (spray, scent, light, vibration, sound, heat) rather than excessively uses the chemicals pesticides.
